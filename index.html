About the board

elecRAY began to come to life in late 2020, when Raman, Akhil and Yuvraj (together as "RAY") began designing the first board that would make up the dream to reality, to be used in thier lifestyle visuals. It took RAY more than a year and a half to complete the board and to make it up their delusional minds.

elecRAY is an emotion with a vintage style, but at the same time it looks extremely modern. The strong bridge between technology and thrill makes it fit not only into the skating community but software engineers as well. Although many of them refuses the idea due to its shift from the traditional messy still cool skates, it got acceptance and lives up to the hype.

It is a purely portable board, ideal for commuting and making others notice you and looks at you twice in that single galnce. It can also used as ice breaker in a conversation(two-way) and making friends. A worthy product for a worthy introvert.

RAY decided to name the board elecRAy because it is all about that electric thrill, an adrenaline rush they get when they are together, which you guys are going to experience once you got your hands on it.
